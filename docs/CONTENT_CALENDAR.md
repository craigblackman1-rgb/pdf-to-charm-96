## CONTENT GUIDELINES

### Writer
- Esther Fair, Level 4 Personal Trainer, Cancer Rehabilitation Specialist
- All posts bylined: "By Esther Fair, Level 4 Personal Trainer, Cancer Rehabilitation Specialist"

### Word Count
- Primary posts (Month 1-3): 1500-2000 words
- Authority posts: 1200-1800 words
- Shorter thought pieces: 800-1200 words

### Structure
1. **Headline** (target keyword included)
2. **Excerpt** (100-150 words, compelling and specific)
3. **Introduction** (Address reader empathy, clarify scope)
4. **3-4 Main Sections** (Build on core question/pain point)
5. **Conclusion** (Summarize + CTA)
6. **Internal Links** (2-3 per post, related content + /personal-training)

### SEO Optimization
- Title includes primary target keyword
- Meta description: 150-160 chars, includes keyword, compelling
- H2/H3 headers use related keywords naturally
- First 100 words answer the main question
- No keyword stuffing

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Link related posts to each other (topic clustering)
- Link all posts to /personal-training with context
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Example anchor: "[cancer rehabilitation training](/personal-training)" not "[click here](/personal-training)"

### Tone & Voice
- Empathetic, not patronizing
- Educational, not promotional
- Specific (use condition names, not "health challenges")
- Reassuring without making medical claims
- Practical and actionable

### Safety
- Include medical disclaimers where appropriate
- Emphasize "always consult your healthcare provider"
- Avoid claiming to diagnose or treat
- Focus on "movement" and "exercise" not "treatment" or "cure"
